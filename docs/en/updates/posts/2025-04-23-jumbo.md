## Jumbo Frame — extended packet rate up to 50 Mbps

In April 2025, OpenIPC added **Jumbo Frame** support in WFB-ng, allowing video streaming at bitrates up to ***50 Mbps***.

This enables even sharper image quality and a more stable video stream during FPV flights.

**How to activate:**
- via OpenIPC Configurator Mario
- or manually: change the jumbo packet rate parameters in `wfb.yaml`

---

**Compatibility**

**According to the author's tests:**

- Jumbo Frame works at **40 MHz** frequency
- Supported only with **RTL8812AU** network cards

**According to OpenFPV authors' tests:**

- Jumbo Frame works at **20 MHz** up to 30 Mbps
- Supported with **RTL8812EU2** network cards
